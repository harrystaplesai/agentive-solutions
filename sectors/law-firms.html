Law Firms

Your solicitors have the capacity. They just cannot bill it because the admin gets in the way.

Unbilled hours

One hour of non-billable admin per solicitor per day across a 10 person team is 50 hours a week. At £250 an hour that is £12,500 a week in capacity the firm has but cannot bill.

The system handles the admin around the billable work. Document chasing, scheduling, client updates. Your solicitors stay on matters.

Enquiry conversion

When someone calls with a new matter and gets a slow response or no follow-up, they call the next firm. If a conveyancing enquiry is worth £3,000 and you lose one a month, that is £36,000 a year. Not because you could not win the work. Because nobody was available.

The system responds to every inbound enquiry within minutes, qualifies the matter, and books a paid consultation.

Client chasing

Clients chase their solicitor for updates. Every inbound chase takes time and creates stress.

The system sends proactive case updates at defined milestones. Clients stay informed. Inbound chases drop. Your solicitors get that time back on files.
