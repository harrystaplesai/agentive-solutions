Mortgage Brokers

Contact clients before their lender does

Monitors your entire client bank. 6 months before each client's fixed rate ends, it sends a personalised WhatsApp message letting them know their deal is ending and asking if they'd like to review their options. Books a review call. Stops you losing clients to lender product transfer offers.

What you get
  • ✓
    Automatic monitoring of all fixed rate end dates Your entire client bank is checked daily. Nothing slips through.
  • ✓
    6-month advance contact Reach clients well before their lender sends a product transfer offer.
  • ✓
    Personalised WhatsApp outreach Each message references the client's name, deal type, and end date. Not a mass blast.
  • ✓
    Review call booking Interested clients get booked straight into your calendar.
