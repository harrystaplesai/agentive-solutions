Agentive OS

Eight agents, one operations team

Each agent has a specific role: responding, scheduling, chasing, drafting. They plug into the tools you already use.

Lead Response

Responds to enquiries within minutes, qualifies the lead, and books a discovery call, even at 11pm on a Sunday.

Inbox Manager

Most of your inbox doesn't need you. This agent handles the routine replies and only passes along what actually requires your brain.

Booking & Scheduling

Confirmations go out instantly. Reminders land at the right time. If someone needs to reschedule, it handles that too, no back and forth.

Review & Reputation

When work is done, your client gets a review request. When reviews come in, you get a drafted response ready to approve or tweak.

Invoice Follow-up

Nobody enjoys chasing money. This agent sends polite reminders that escalate gradually, so you don't have to have the awkward conversation.

Quote & Proposal

Leave a voice note describing what the client needs. You get a properly formatted proposal back, built from your pricing and templates. Send it or adjust it.

Client Onboarding

Triggers a welcome sequence: confirmation email, contract, intake form, and kickoff booking. All automatically.

Weekly Ops Briefing

Every Monday you get a clear summary of what happened, what's coming up, and what's slipped. No digging through apps to piece it together.
